Key System Capabilities

Multi-Vector Threat Detection

Identifies Volumetric DDoS attacks, SYN & UDP Port Scans, SSH/FTP Brute Force sequences, Trojan Backdoor ports, and Zero-Day Anomaly outliers.

Unsupervised Machine Learning

Trains on packet size, protocol numerical ID, source/destination ports, and arrival time delta to detect unclassified zero-day exploits.

Automated Containment

Auto-blocks high & critical severity threat IPs with customizable expiration windows and full administrative audit logging.
